🌤️ Weather Data Formatting Fix

This page demonstrates the fix for excessive decimal places in weather data display.

🔍 Issues Fixed

  • Wind Speed: "11.122774053213258 km/h" → "11.1 km/h"
  • Humidity: "73.50998077401059%" → "74%"
  • UV Index: Ensured whole numbers (0-15 range)
  • Pressure: Ensured whole numbers (e.g., "1013 hPa")
  • Temperature: Ensured whole numbers (e.g., "25°C")

📊 Before vs After Comparison

❌ BEFORE (Excessive Decimals)

🌬️ Wind Speed: 11.122774053213258 km/h
💧 Humidity: 73.50998077401059%
☀️ UV Index: 5.234567
🌡️ Temperature: 24.789123°C
📊 Pressure: 1013.456789 hPa

⚠️ Confusing and hard to read for users

✅ AFTER (Clean Format)

🌬️ Wind Speed: 11.1 km/h
💧 Humidity: 74%
☀️ UV Index: 5
🌡️ Temperature: 25°C
📊 Pressure: 1013 hPa

✅ Clean, readable, and user-friendly
