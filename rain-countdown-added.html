How Rain Countdown Works

Rain Detection Logic:

  • Analyzes hourly weather data for precipitation
  • Looks for precipitation type > 0 OR precipitation probability > 70%
  • Finds the next rain event in the forecast
  • Calculates time until rain starts and expected duration
